This is an easy chocolate cookie recipe

Ingredients

  • 120g butter
  • 75g light brown sugar
  • 75g golden caster sugar
  • 1 medium egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 180g plain flour
  • 1/2 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 150g dark chocolate, cut into chunkz

Method

This is an easy step choc chip cookie guideline

  1. Step 1
    • Heat over to 180C/150C fan/gas 4
    • Line 2 baking sheets with patchment
    • Cream the butter and sugars together until very light
    • Combine egg and vanilla
    • Stir in flour, bicarb, chocolate and 1/4 tsp salt
  1. Step 2
    • Scoop 10 large tbsps of the mixture onto trays
    • Bake for 10-12 mins or until firm
    • Leave to cool on the tray for a few mins
